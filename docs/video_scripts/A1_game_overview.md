# VIDEO A1 — Game Overview

**Duration:** 25–30 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

Opens the instruction sequence. Tells the participant: you will play 3 seasons (1 practice, 2 real). Real rounds earn real money.

---

## Narration Script (~50 words, pace: slow and warm)

> "Welcome to the farming game! You will play three seasons.
>
> The first season is just for practice — it does not count.
>
> The next two seasons are real. The tokens you earn in those seasons will be converted to real money that you take home today."

---

## Storyboard

| Seconds | Visual Description |
|---------|-------------------|
| 0–3 | Fade in on a bright, flat-style African maize field under a warm blue sky. A simple farmer figure stands at the field edge. Calm, inviting. |
| 3–10 | Three circular season icons appear in a horizontal row above the field: (1) a small green seedling labeled with a practice/training symbol, (2) a golden corn stalk, (3) another golden corn stalk. They appear one at a time, left to right. |
| 10–16 | The seedling icon dims and a subtle "PRACTICE" tag appears on it. The two corn stalk icons brighten. Small gold coin icons float up from each of the two real-round icons. |
| 16–24 | Close-up: gold coins stack up and then visually transform into paper currency (generic bills, no specific denomination). A friendly visual beat — the message is "tokens become real money." |
| 24–30 | Pull back to the full field scene. All three season icons are visible. The two real-round icons glow gold. Gentle fade to a calm end frame. |

---

## Visual Requirements

- Farmer figure: simple, dignified, wearing casual clothing and a sun hat. No stereotyping.
- Season icons: clean circular badges, easily distinguishable at tablet arm's length.
- Gold coins: round, bright, unmistakable. The defining visual motif of the entire game.
- Mood: welcoming, non-intimidating. This is the first thing a nervous participant sees.

---

## Audio

- Narration recorded separately in each deployment language (English, Luganda, Bemba, Nyanja)
- No background music. Clean voice only.
- If using AI audio generation (e.g., Veo 3.1 native audio), include the narration text in the prompt.
