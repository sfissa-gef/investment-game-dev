# VIDEO A5 — Fertilizer Payout: Bad Rain

**Duration:** 20–25 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

Teaches: if rain is bad, all fertilizer investment is lost — 0 tokens back. But saved tokens in the lockbox are safe.

**Critical numbers that MUST appear on screen:** 1 (cost), 0 (return).

---

## Narration Script (~45 words)

> "But if the rains are bad, your crops fail.
>
> No matter how much fertilizer you bought, you get 0 tokens back from your harvest.
>
> However, the tokens you did not spend — the tokens in your lockbox — are still yours. They are safe."
