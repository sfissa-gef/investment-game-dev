# VIDEO B2 — Insurance Explanation: Version B (Bundled)

**Duration:** 28–30 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

This is a **core research instrument.** Participants pay 1 token to watch it. It explains how insurance works when seeds and insurance are offered as a **single bundled product** (Version B of the experiment).

The video must communicate: the bundle costs 12 tokens (10 for seeds + 2 for insurance), good rain pays 30, bad rain pays 10 (insurance kicks in).

**The video must NOT advocate for or against buying the bundle.** Neutral, factual presentation only.

---

## Critical Numbers (MUST appear on screen)

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Bundle cost (seeds + insurance) | 12 tokens |
| — of which seeds | 10 tokens |
| — of which insurance | 2 tokens |
| Good rain — bundle payout | 30 tokens |
| Bad rain — seed payout | 0 tokens |
| Bad rain — insurance payout | 10 tokens |

---

## Narration Script (~55 words, pace ~120 wpm)

> "The improved seeds cost 12 tokens. This price includes insurance — 10 tokens for the seeds and 2 tokens for the insurance.
>
> If there is good rain: you earn 30 tokens. The insurance pays nothing.
>
> If there is bad rain: your crop fails. But the insurance pays you back 10 tokens, covering the seed cost.
>
> Either way, you get something back."

## Visual Requirements

- **The bundle must look like ONE product** — seeds with an insurance badge attached, not two separate items sitting next to each other. This is the key visual difference from Video B1.
- **The price breakdown (10 + 2 = 12)** should be shown briefly but clearly — it reveals that part of the price is insurance, which the participant may not have realized from the game screen alone.
- **Scenes 2–4** are structurally identical to Video B1 scenes 2–4. The payoff numbers are the same. Only the framing in Scene 1 differs (bundled vs. unbundled). This parallelism is intentional.
- All numbers must be readable at arm's length on a 10-inch tablet.

---

## Audio

- Narration only, no music.
- Pace: steady 120 wpm. Clear pauses after each number.
- Tone: neutral, informational. Same energy as Video B1.
- On "this price includes insurance" — slight emphasis, as this is the key information the participant might not have realized.

## Research Integrity Note

This video is the **bundled** counterpart to Video B1 (unbundled). The payoff numbers are identical. The ONLY difference is the framing:

| | Video B1 (Unbundled) | Video B2 (Bundled) |
|---|---|---|
| **Scene 1** | Seeds (10) + Insurance (2) shown as separate items | Seeds+Insurance shown as one product (12), then decomposed |
| **Scenes 2–4** | Identical | Identical |

This difference in framing IS the experimental treatment. Do not alter the visual structure of Scene 1 without consulting the research team, as it directly affects the research questions about how bundling affects comprehension and demand.
