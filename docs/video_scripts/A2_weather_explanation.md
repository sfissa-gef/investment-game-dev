# VIDEO A2 — Weather Explanation

**Duration:** 25–30 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

Teaches the participant that weather is random: good rain occurs 4 out of 5 seasons, bad rain (drought) occurs 1 out of 5. Bad rain can happen two seasons in a row.

---

## Narration Script (~55 words)

> "Your harvest depends on the rains.
>
> Most seasons — four out of five — there will be good rains, and your crops will grow well.
>
> But sometimes — one out of five — there is bad rain, a drought, and your crops fail.
>
> You cannot predict which season will have bad rain. It is random, just like real weather."

---

## Storyboard

| Seconds | Visual Description |
|---------|-------------------|
| 0–4 | Fade in on a split screen. Left side is larger (roughly 80% of frame), showing a blue rain cloud with 3 falling droplets above a lush green maize field with golden tassels. Bright, healthy, optimistic. |
| 4–10 | Below the left scene, four blue circles light up in a row of five, representing "4 out of 5." Each circle lights up sequentially with a gentle pulse. |
| 10–16 | Right side of the split (roughly 20% of frame) expands to equal size. Shows an empty cloud outline (no rain) above cracked brown earth with wilted yellow stalks. Muted, somber but NOT scary. |
| 16–22 | Below the right scene, one brown/tan circle lights up in the fifth position of the same row of five. The contrast is clear: 4 blue vs. 1 brown. |
| 22–30 | Both scenes remain visible side-by-side. A simple visual metaphor for randomness: a bag or container with 5 marbles (4 blue, 1 brown) gently shakes, and one marble rolls out randomly. Fade out. |

## AI Video Generation Prompt

```
2D flat animation, split-screen educational style, warm bright colors.

Left side (taking up most of the frame): a friendly blue rain cloud with three 
water droplets falling onto a lush green maize field with golden corn tassels. 
Healthy, abundant crops. Below: four blue circles in a horizontal row of five 
light up one by one.

Right side: an empty cloud outline with no rain drops, above cracked dry brown 
earth with thin wilted yellow maize stalks. Below: one brown circle lights up 
as the fifth dot in the row.

The split screen shows clear contrast between good rain (abundant) and bad rain 
(drought). The drought side is matter-of-fact, not dramatic or frightening.

At the end, a small visual of a bag with 5 marbles (4 blue, 1 brown) gently 
shakes to show randomness.

Style: clean flat vector animation, like an agricultural NGO educational video. 
Colors: rain blue (#90CAF9), field green (#7CB342), drought brown (#A1887F), 
cracked earth tan (#D7CCC8). Static camera. Duration: 28 seconds. 16:9 aspect.
```

---

## Visual Requirements

- The 4-out-of-5 / 1-out-of-5 visual must be unmistakable even without narration.
- Drought imagery: dry, cracked, but NOT apocalyptic. No dead animals, no distressed people.
- Good rain imagery: abundant but not fantastical. Realistic lush crops.
- The marble/randomness metaphor should be simple and brief (5–8 seconds max).

---

## Audio

- Narration only, no background music or sound effects.
- Pace: especially slow on "four out of five" and "one out of five" — these are the critical numbers.
