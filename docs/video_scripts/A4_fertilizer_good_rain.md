# VIDEO A4 — Fertilizer Payout: Good Rain

**Duration:** 20–25 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

Teaches: each token spent on fertilizer returns 2 tokens when rain is good. Doubling your investment.

**Critical numbers that MUST appear on screen:** 1 (cost), 2 (return), 10 (max investment), 20 (max return).

---

## Narration Script (~45 words)

> "You can buy fertilizer for your farm. Each bag of fertilizer costs 1 token.
>
> If the rains are good, each bag gives you 2 tokens back — double your investment!
>
> If you buy all 10 bags, and the rains are good, you will get 20 tokens as your harvest."
