# VIDEO A3 — Budget Explanation

**Duration:** 20–25 seconds | **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape | **Resolution:** 1280×720 | **Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Purpose

Teaches: each round you get 25 tokens. Tokens are your budget. Unspent tokens are saved. Tokens do NOT carry over between rounds.

---

## Narration Script (~45 words)

> "Each season, you receive 25 tokens. These are your budget.
>
> You can use tokens to buy farming inputs. Any tokens you do not spend are saved — they stay in your lockbox.
>
> Each season you get 25 fresh tokens. They do not carry over from one season to the next."

---

## Storyboard

| Seconds | Visual Description |
|---------|-------------------|
| 0–4 | A black metal lockbox sits centered on a simple wooden surface, warm cream background. 25 gold coins cascade into the lockbox from above in a satisfying stream. The number "25" appears large below the lockbox. |
| 4–10 | Some coins (e.g., 7) float out of the lockbox toward a green fertilizer bag on the right, leaving 18 in the box. The lockbox number updates from "25" to "18". The message: tokens leave to buy things, remainder stays. |
| 10–16 | A visual divider or "NEW SEASON" banner sweeps across the screen. The lockbox empties completely (coins fade away). |
| 16–22 | 25 new coins cascade back into the lockbox. The number resets to "25". The message: fresh budget each season, nothing carries over. |
| 22–25 | End frame: lockbox with "25", calm and clear. |

---

## Visual Requirements

- The coin cascade should feel satisfying and tangible — coins have weight and momentum.
- The "25" number must be very large and readable from arm's length on a tablet.
- The "new season / reset" moment must be visually clear — tokens don't carry over is a critical rule.
- Lockbox design should match the app's in-game lockbox icon for visual consistency.

---

## Audio

- Narration only.
- Optional: subtle coin-clinking sound effect during the cascade (very soft, not distracting).
