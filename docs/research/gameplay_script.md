Gameplay layout:

Explanatory content (video/audio):

- This is a farming game, which will ask you to make some investments
  just as you would in real life.

- We will explain the rules of the game to you. Then you will have the
  opportunity to play a practice round. Then you will play 2 “real”
  rounds. You can think of each round like a separate agricultural
  season. You will get payouts in real life that correspond to your
  harvests in the game for the two real rounds.

- Your harvest depends on the rains. In the game, most of the time there
  will be “good rains”, in which crops will yield well. But sometimes,
  there are “bad rains” in which your crop totally fails. About 1 in
  every 5 seasons there will be bad rain. However, it is still possible
  to get bad rain two seasons in a row, just like in the real world.

- In each round, you will get a budget of 25 tokens. Each token is worth
  XX local currency. You can use these tokens to invest in inputs for
  your farm. The budget does not get carried over between rounds. You
  get 25 new tokens each round.

- At first, you will plant local maize seeds. You can choose to use up
  to 10 tokens to invest in fertilizer for your farm.

- Any tokens you don’t invest will be saved and added to your payout at
  harvest.

- If the rain is good, fertilizer increases the amount of maize produced
  that you can sell. So for each investment of 1 token, you will get
  double that amount in outputs if the rain is good. For example, if you
  invest 1 token in fertilizer and the rain is good, then you will get 2
  tokens as a harvest. If you invest all 10 tokens in fertilizer, and
  the rains are good, you will get 20 tokens as a harvest!

- However, if the rain is bad, your crops fail. You will get 0 tokens as
  a harvest no matter how much money you spent on fertilizer.

- You will know that there was good rain if you see this image of a
  bumper harvest \[show\] and a bad rain if you see this image of a
  failed harvest \[show\].

Practice round:

- This round is just for practice so that you can understand how to play
  the game.

- Your 25 tokens are placed in the lockbox. You can spend up to 10 on
  fertilizer, by pressing the “plus” button under the fertilizer bag.
  The number of tokens left over in your lockbox will update based on
  the amount spent on fertilizer.

- You do not have to buy any fertilizer if you don’t want to. In that
  case, do not press the plus button.

- After you have finished choosing how much fertilizer to use and are
  ready to plant your seeds, touch the plant button in the lower right
  corner.

- Do not press that button until you are sure you are finished, as you
  cannot change your mind after pressing that button.

Round 2 \[Version 1\]:

- You now have one more season to play. This time will also matter for
  your payment that you will get after the exercise is over. For each
  token you have at harvest, you will get \[XX currency\] after the game
  is over.

- But in this round, your decision is a little bit more complicated! You
  are still able to purchase fertilizer, but now can also purchase
  improved seeds to plant instead of local seeds, and you can also
  purchase insurance for those seeds if you’d like.

- The improved seeds cost 10 tokens. If there is good rain, and you
  plant the improved seeds you will get 30 tokens, in addition to any
  tokens you get from buying fertilizer. But if there is bad rain,
  similarly to the fertilizer, you will get 0 tokens, as the crop has
  failed.

- You can also buy insurance for the seeds for 2 tokens more. This will
  help you to recoup the cost of your seed investment if there is bad
  rain.

- Would you like to learn more about insurance before you make your
  decision? Learning more about the insurance costs 1 token. That is, if
  you choose to watch the video, you will instead start the season with
  24 tokens to purchase inputs instead of 25. If you would like to pay 1
  token to watch the video, tap the “hands” icon to learn more! \[If
  selected, play video then return.\]

- If you would like to proceed to the decision without learning more,
  simply tap the arrow button.

> Video content \[V1\]:

- Purchasing improved seeds has the potential to greatly increase your
  yields and earnings.

- However, investing in improved seeds is also risky! If there is a
  drought, then your harvest might fail. Then you will lose all the
  money you invested in the seeds, and you will be worse off than if you
  had just saved your money.

- Insurance can help making investing in seeds less risky. How does it
  work?

- If you choose to buy insurance, this is what happens: You will pay 2
  tokens up front at the beginning of the season. This is in addition to
  the 10 tokens you are spending on seeds, for 12 tokens total.

- If there is good rain, the seeds will pay off 30 tokens. The insurance
  will not pay you back anything.

- If there is bad rain, the seeds will pay off 0. But the good news is
  that the insurance will kick in and pay you back 10 tokens, covering
  the initial price of the seeds. So even if there is bad rain you will
  still get something back!

- Paying for insurance is like paying for peace of mind. While you won’t
  get anything back if there is good rain, your harvest returns will be
  so high that you probably won’t miss those 2 tokens that you paid! But
  in a drought, you won’t be left with nothing.
